# Candace Knowledge Graph

A searchable, interconnected knowledge base extracted from The Candace Owens Show.

## Browse

- **[Episodes](./episodes)** - Full transcripts with timestamped references
- **[Entities](./entities)** - People, organizations, places, and concepts
- **[Timeline](./index/timeline.md)** - Events in chronological order
- **[Claims](./index/claims.md)** - Factual claims with verification status

## Features

- **Click timestamps** to see all entities, events, and claims at that moment
- **Hover over links** to preview content
- **Use the graph** to explore connections
- **Search** to find anything

## Stats

This knowledge graph currently contains transcripts and extracted data from multiple episodes, including hundreds of entities, events, and claims.
